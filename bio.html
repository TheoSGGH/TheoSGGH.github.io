About Me

Theo is an 18 year old student at San Jose State University, where he is majoring in Digital Media art. He enjoys creating somewhat vapor-wave, 80's esque art using photography for refrence. In his free time, you can make a safe bet that he is either at home playing video games or live streaming on his twitch channel, Limbonium. He hopes to expand his horizons on what types of art he enjoys to create and what new skills he can pick up along the way!
